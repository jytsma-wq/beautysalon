# Local SERP Competitor Analysis

Date: 2026-07-04
Phase: 7 - Competitor and SERP analysis
Branch: `codex/visibility-readiness-audit`
Mode: public search-result observations only; no scraping campaign; no code changes

## Safety Rules Applied

- No competitor content was copied.
- No aggressive scraping was performed.
- No competitor terms were bypassed.
- No public listings, profiles, reviews, or site content were edited.
- No DNS, Hostinger, analytics, ads, billing, or production code changes were made.
- Findings are approximate because rankings vary by location, language, personalization, device, and time.

## Search Context And Limitations

Public query samples were checked across English, Georgian, Russian, Turkish, Arabic, and Hebrew using public web search observations.

Limitations:

- Google Maps/local pack results were not reliably accessible from this environment with a fixed Batumi/Georgia location context.
- Google Business Profile strength was inferred only from public search snippets and public map/directory surfaces where available.
- Review counts and ratings are approximate and source-specific; a salon can have different ratings on Google, Yandex, Tripadvisor, 100doc, Salonly, and directories.
- Local pack checks should be repeated manually in Google Search/Maps from Batumi or with a controlled Georgia location context.

## Major Competitors

### LAKmousse Beauty Bar

Public evidence:

- Main site describes a central Batumi beauty salon with a large service catalog, service/prices links, gallery, FAQ, WhatsApp/Telegram/social links, and daily opening hours.
- Public snippets and the site describe hundreds of services and a broad beauty-space positioning.
- LAKmousse catalog exposes service names and prices such as acne treatment and multiple aesthetic/beauty services.
- Yandex Maps shows LAKmousse with rating around `5.0`, `85 ratings`, `65 reviews`, and around `140` photos.
- Tripadvisor appears for LAKmousse in hair/nail salon and spa-related result sets with roughly `4.0-4.1` rating and around `18-20` reviews depending on snippet.

Strengths:

- Strong service breadth.
- Clear prices across many services.
- Strong map/directory footprint.
- Many public photos/reviews.
- Strong Russian/English visibility.
- Tripadvisor/Yandex support local and tourist discovery.

Silk gap:

- Silk has better dedicated local SEO pages for some intents, but weaker external citation and review footprint.
- Silk needs more public photos, confirmed GBP/Bing/Yandex listings, and stronger directory presence.

### Academy of Cosmetology

Public evidence:

- Appears for Botox, cosmetology, and Russian aesthetic queries.
- Site positions it as a cosmetology/dermatology/training center.
- 100doc listing shows around `30 reviews`.
- Public snippets show Botox, peels, consultation, dermatology/cosmetology themes.
- Russian Botox pages expose detailed price tables in search snippets.

Strengths:

- Strong topical authority for Botox/cosmetology.
- Training/academy positioning can signal expertise.
- Review/directory presence via 100doc.
- Service-specific pages and price visibility.

Silk gap:

- Silk should not invent credentials, but should make verified practitioner expertise, consultation process, safety, aftercare, and service pricing more visible.
- Silk needs credible third-party profiles and reviews to compete in medical-aesthetic queries.

### Heaven Nails Studio

- Yandex Maps shows around `5.0`, `144 ratings`, `122 reviews`, and around `137` photos.

Strengths:

- Strong nail/lash/brow category focus.
- Very strong map photo/review footprint.

- Clear niche positioning.

Silk gap:

- Silk has nail/lash/brow pages, but needs stronger photos, review signals, and listing/category alignment for those services.

## Review Opportunities

Do:

- Ask real clients for honest reviews after completed appointments.
- Use one privacy-safe follow-up message.
- Reply to reviews without revealing treatment details.
- Track review requests privately, not review content.

Do not:

- Buy reviews.
- Offer discounts/incentives for reviews.
- Review-gate unhappy clients.
- Add review/aggregateRating schema without compliant third-party data and owner approval.

Priority review surfaces:

- Google Business Profile.
- Salonly, if booking flow is active and accurate.
- Yandex Maps, if owner approves listing management.
- Tripadvisor, if owner approves a tourist-facing listing.
- Facebook page recommendations, if active.
